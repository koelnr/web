# Refund and Cancellation Policy

_Last updated: February 7, 2026_

This policy describes cancellation, rescheduling, and refund terms for koelnr services.

## 1. One-Time Bookings

- Cancellations made before service partner assignment may be eligible for full refund.
- Late cancellations or no-shows may incur charges based on operational costs.

## 2. Subscription Services

- Subscription benefits are delivered as per monthly cycle and plan terms.
- Unused monthly slots do not automatically carry forward unless explicitly stated in plan terms.

## 3. Rescheduling

- Users can request rescheduling through the app or support channels.
- Rescheduling is subject to slot availability.

## 4. Failed Service Attempts

If service cannot be completed due to operational reasons from koelnr, users may receive rescheduling or eligible credit/refund.

## 5. Refund Timelines

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method and may take 5-10 business days depending on the payment provider.

## 6. Exceptions

Promotional or discounted bookings may have separate refund conditions shown at checkout.

## 7. Contact

For cancellation or refund support, contact [support@koelnr.com](mailto:support@koelnr.com).
