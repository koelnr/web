# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: February 7, 2026_

This Privacy Policy explains how koelnr collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our app, website, and related services.

## 1. Information We Collect

- Account details such as name, phone number, and email.
- Vehicle and service details such as address, preferred slots, and booking history.
- Payment-related metadata from payment providers (we do not store full card details).
- Device and usage information such as app logs, IP address, and diagnostics.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

- Deliver and manage bookings and subscriptions.
- Provide customer support and quality assurance.
- Process billing, invoices, and refunds.
- Improve product features, reliability, and service quality.
- Send service notifications, reminders, and important policy updates.

## 3. Sharing of Information

We share information only when required to operate our services:

- Service partners and field operations teams for booking fulfillment.
- Payment processors for transaction handling.
- Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law.

We do not sell your personal data.

## 4. Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for operational, legal, and accounting purposes.

## 5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data, subject to legal limitations.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 18.

## 8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated through app or website notices.

## 9. Contact

For privacy requests or concerns, contact us at [support@koelnr.com](mailto:support@koelnr.com).
